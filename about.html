About Me

I'm a Product Designer at Realtor's Property Resource, where I specialize in crafting mobile-first experiences for real estate professionals. My career began in Graphic Design, but I quickly realized I wanted to do more than make things look good—I wanted to understand what makes them work.

This curiosity evolved into a deep passion for UX, where I leverage my background in Visual Communication from San Jose State University (Go Spartans!) to turn complex problems into intuitive solutions. From my time at NortonLifeLock and New American Funding to my current work, I’ve embraced a "jack of all trades" mindset—leading projects from the first research interview to production-ready handoffs. My goal is always the same: to strip away the mundane and create technology that feels human, helpful, and memorable.
